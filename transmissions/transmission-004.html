TRANSMISSION LOG

The air didn’t move much today.

Not in a way I could really explain clearly.

It just stayed in place.

Even outside, everything felt like it was holding its position a bit longer than usual.

There were sounds, but they didn’t feel carried by anything.

Like they were just happening where they were born, and not traveling anywhere after that.

I kept looking outside for a while without really thinking about anything specific.

Just watching.

Waiting for something to shift, even slightly.

But nothing really did.

No wind. No noticeable change in direction of the air.

Just stillness with noise inside it.

At some point, there was rain.

It didn’t build up.

It just arrived.

Quick and uneven.

Like it didn’t have much time to stay.

After a short while, it stopped again.

The ground barely had time to react.

Later, I heard that other places experienced flooding from the same kind of rainfall.

Not long events.

Just sudden ones.

Short bursts that became too much in certain areas.

It felt strange hearing that while everything here stayed mostly unchanged.

Like the weather wasn’t happening evenly across the same moment.

Just different outcomes depending on where it landed.

I stayed inside for most of the day after that.

The room felt quieter than usual.

Not because it was silent, but because nothing felt like it was being carried in from outside.

At one point, I opened the window, but it didn’t really change much.

The air outside and inside felt similar.

Just different temperatures of the same stillness.

Eventually, I closed it again.

There wasn’t anything urgent about the day.

Just a steady lack of movement in things that usually move without being noticed.

It’s hard to tell if that means something is changing.

Or if I’m just noticing things differently now.

For now…

continuity remains uncertain.
